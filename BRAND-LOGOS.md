## Where to Get Official Logos

### Option 1: Brand Websites (Preferred)
Visit each brand's official website and look for:
- Press kit / Media kit
- About page
- Logo downloads

### Option 2: Contact Brands Directly
Email brands for permission to use logos on your e-commerce site

### Option 3: BigCommerce Product Images
Extract logos from existing product images in your BigCommerce catalog

## Logo Specifications

**Recommended:**
- Format: PNG with transparent background
- Size: 300x200px (will be responsive)
- Max file size: 50KB per logo
- Color: Full color (original brand colors)

## Quick Setup

1. Create `images/brands/` folder in your repo
2. Download logos from brand websites
3. Rename to match the filenames above
4. Logos will automatically appear in the mockup!
